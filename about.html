About Me

Her family and friends call her “Abby”. She has been living in Bulacan for 21 years where her parents' province is from. She wants to be successful in her life to provide for the needs of her family. Her love for her family is unconditional. She was raised by her mother while her father is an OFW, she is a second child among her other siblings which are her older sister and younger twin brothers. She is very close with her family; they always bond with her siblings by playing online games and the closeness with her mother is unpredictable. Her parents are always proud and supportive of her decisions in life.

INTEREST

Abigail Joy has a deep love for music and concerts. She thoroughly enjoys the experience of being surrounded by live music, feeling the crowd's energy, and being transported by the performances on stage. Attending concerts allows her to unwind, embrace the moment, and immerse herself in the music.

She has been an avid fan of K-pop since 2013, and her love for boy groups for a long time is unbelievable. Attending K-pop concerts and fan events has become a regular part of her life, and she saves up diligently to be able to afford tickets and exclusive merchandise. Her love for K-pop has also sparked friendships with fellow fans, as they bond over their shared interests and attend fan gatherings together, even making an event for their idols. It's truly amazing to witness the level of dedication and passion she has for the K-pop world.

Their music provided solace, motivation, and distraction from the challenges caused by the pandemic.
